Different Types of Roles in MLBB

Ruby (EXP)
Ruby is an EXP laner who excels in sustain and crowd control.
Ling (Jungle)
Ling is a jungle assassin known for high mobility and burst damage.
Valentina (Mid/Mage)
Valentina is a mage/mid laner who excels in stealing enemy ultimates.
Beatrix (Marksman)
Beatrix is a marksman with versatile weapon options for high damage output.
Chou (Tank)
Chou is a tank who specializes in disruption and initiation.
